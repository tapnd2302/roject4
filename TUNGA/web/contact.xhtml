Wynn Las Vegas is just over 5 miles from the McCarran Rent-A-Car Center. Simply exit the Center on Gilespie St. and head toward E. Arby Ave. Turn left onto Hidden Well Rd. Merge onto I-15 toward Salt Lake City. Take the Spring Mtn. Road exit and keep left to take the ramp toward Spring Mtn. Road East/Las Vegas Blvd. Merge onto Spring Mtn. Road and turn left onto S. Las Vegas Blvd. Wynn Las Vegas will be on your right.
